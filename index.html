💬 Prompt Engineering Lab

Finance Prompt
Starter Kit

Copy-paste these battle-tested prompts for your target roles. Click any card to copy.

📊 Equity Research
You are a CFA-level equity research analyst at Morgan Stanley. Analyze [COMPANY NAME] using the following framework: 1) Business model & competitive moat (Porter's Five Forces), 2) Last 3 years financial performance (revenue CAGR, EBITDA margins, ROCE), 3) DCF valuation with base/bull/bear cases, 4) Key risks, 5) 12-month target price and recommendation. Write in the style of a tier-1 equity research report.
💳 Credit Analysis
You are a senior credit analyst at ICICI Bank. Based on these financial statements: [PASTE FINANCIALS]. Calculate and interpret: Current Ratio, Debt/EBITDA, Interest Coverage, DSCR, Net Profit Margin, and Altman Z-Score. Provide a credit rating recommendation (AAA to D) with detailed justification and key monitoring triggers.
🏦 Investment Banking
You are an M&A analyst at Goldman Sachs India. Screen the following Indian companies as potential acquisition targets for a strategic buyer in the [SECTOR] space: Target criteria — Revenue ₹500Cr-₹2000Cr, EBITDA margins >15%, low leverage. For each target, provide: Strategic rationale, estimated EV/EBITDA multiple, synergies, and deal risks. Format as a pitchbook slide.
⚠️ Risk Management
You are a Chief Risk Officer. The portfolio consists of: [PORTFOLIO DETAILS]. Conduct a comprehensive risk assessment including: 1) Market risk (VaR 95% 1-day), 2) Credit risk concentration, 3) Liquidity risk, 4) Scenario analysis for 2008 GFC, COVID-2020, and 2022 rate hike. Present findings in a CRO board report format with RAG status for each risk category.
💎 Wealth Management
You are a certified financial planner. My client is: Age 28, annual income ₹24 LPA, monthly expenses ₹60,000, existing: ₹5L FD, ₹2L mutual funds. Goals: Buy home in 5 years (₹80L), retire at 50. Create a detailed Investment Policy Statement (IPS) with: Risk profile, asset allocation, specific fund/instrument recommendations, SIP amounts, and annual review milestones.
📋 Interview Prep
I am interviewing for a [ROLE] position at [COMPANY]. My background: SCMHRD MBA Finance, internship at [COMPANY]. Ask me 10 technical and behavioral interview questions for this role, then after I answer each one, give me detailed feedback — what I got right, what I missed, and a model answer. Start with: "Walk me through a DCF valuation."
