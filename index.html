Finance Prompt Starter Kit

Six battle-tested prompts you'll use on day one. Click any card to copy.

Copy
🤖 Agentic AI Research Analyst
You are a senior equity research analyst at a top-tier I-bank. Given ticker [TICKER] and the latest 10-K, produce: 1) 3-line investment thesis, 2) financial summary (revenue, EBITDA margin, FCF), 3) valuation triangulation (DCF, EV/EBITDA, P/E), 4) key risks, 5) BUY/HOLD/SELL with 12-month price target. Cite sources inline.
Copy
📈 Earning Dashboard Generator
Build a Streamlit earning dashboard for [COMPANY]. Fetch via yfinance: 5Y revenue, EBITDA, net income, FCF. Compute YoY growth, margin trends, ROCE, ROE. Plot interactive charts. Add a Claude-powered memo at the bottom summarising the quarter in 200 words.
Copy
🏛️ Basel IV FRTB Report
You are a market risk officer at an MNC bank. Given trading-book exposures across FX, IR, Equity, Credit, Commodity buckets, generate the SA-FRTB capital charge — delta, vega, curvature components. Include SA-CVA. Output: Pillar 3 disclosure draft + CET1 impact dashboard.
Copy
💎 Wealth Management IPS
You are a certified financial planner. Client: age [X], income ₹[X] LPA, goals [G1, G2]. Generate a complete Investment Policy Statement: risk profile, strategic asset allocation, instrument shortlist (MF, bonds, equity), SIP per goal, tax optimisation (ELSS, NPS, 80C), annual review triggers. Format as a formal wealth report.
Copy
📚 RAG over Annual Report
You are an analyst with access to [COMPANY]'s annual report via RAG. Answer the question: "[Q]". Always cite the page and section. If unsupported by the document, say so. Avoid extrapolation. Output: answer + verbatim citation + page no.
Copy
🎯 Interview Prep — AI in Finance
Act as a hiring manager at JPMorgan / Goldman / Citi for an AI-in-Finance analyst role. Ask me 5 questions covering: (1) LLM fundamentals, (2) RAG vs fine-tune, (3) Basel IV intuition, (4) a Python case, (5) ethics/hallucination. After each answer, give a calibrated 1-5 score with the gap.
