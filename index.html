🧠 Core AI Concepts

LLM · RAG · Context
& MCP — Explained

The four pillars of Module 1 — demystified for finance professionals.

🧠
Large Language Model
A neural network trained on trillions of tokens that can understand and generate human language. Powered by transformers — the architecture behind GPT-4, Claude, and Bloomberg GPT. Knows finance from training data but can hallucinate facts.
Finance use: Earnings summarization, DCF commentary, credit memo drafting
📚
RAG — Retrieval Augmented Generation
Grounds LLM responses in real, up-to-date data. A retrieval layer fetches relevant documents from a vector database, then injects them into the prompt as context. Morgan Stanley uses RAG to let advisors query 100K+ research reports.
Finance use: Annual report Q&A, prospectus analysis, live market data retrieval
🔍
Context Window
The maximum text an LLM can "see" at once — like working memory. Claude has 200K tokens (~150K words), enough to process an entire annual report. Context management determines output quality more than prompt wording alone.
Finance use: Full 10-K analysis, multi-year financial comparison, deal room docs
⚙️
Model Context Protocol (MCP)
Anthropic's open standard for connecting AI models to external tools, databases, and APIs without custom code. Like USB-C for AI — one protocol to plug an LLM into Bloomberg Terminal, Excel, a risk database, or a trading platform.
Finance use: Live data feeds, automated report generation, agentic deal workflows

💬 Prompt Engineering Lab

Finance Prompt
Starter Kit

Battle-tested prompts for your target role. Click to copy.

🧠 LLM + RAG — Annual Report Q&A
You are a senior credit analyst. I will provide an annual report extract below. Answer the following questions using ONLY information from the document: 1) What is the Debt/EBITDA ratio for the last 3 years? 2) List all disclosed covenant risks. 3) Summarize liquidity position. 4) Flag any red flags a lender should be aware of. Annual Report: [PASTE TEXT]
🏦 Investment Banking — Pitchbook Section
You are an M&A analyst at Goldman Sachs India. Generate a "Transaction Rationale" slide for the following deal: Acquirer: [NAME], Target: [NAME], Sector: [SECTOR]. Include: Strategic fit (3 bullets), synergy estimates (revenue + cost), key risks (3), and comparable transactions. Format as concise bullet points for a pitchbook slide.
⚙️ MCP Pattern — Agentic Finance Workflow
You are an AI agent with access to the following tools: [get_stock_data(ticker)], [get_news(company, days=7)], [calculate_dcf(revenue_growth, margins, wacc)]. Task: Analyze [COMPANY] for a buy/sell decision. Step 1: Fetch 3-year financials. Step 2: Get recent news sentiment. Step 3: Run DCF with base/bull/bear assumptions. Step 4: Output a 1-page investment memo.
📋 Credit Analysis — RAG Memo
You are a credit analyst at ICICI Bank with access to the company's financial statements below. Compute: Current Ratio, Quick Ratio, Debt/EBITDA, Interest Coverage, DSCR, and Altman Z-Score. For each ratio: state the value, industry benchmark, and risk implication. Conclude with a credit rating recommendation (AAA to D) and 3 monitoring triggers. Financials: [PASTE DATA]
⚠️ Risk Management — Stress Test Narrative
You are a Chief Risk Officer preparing a board report. Portfolio: [DESCRIBE]. Run a narrative stress test for these scenarios: 1) 2008 GFC — credit spreads +400bps, equity -50%, 2) COVID-2020 — liquidity shock, 3) 2022 rate hike — rates +500bps in 12 months. For each: estimated P&L impact, key risk drivers, and 3 mitigation actions. Output in board-ready language with RAG (Red/Amber/Green) status.
🎯 Interview Prep — AI Finance Role Play
I am interviewing for [ROLE] at [COMPANY]. Ask me technical interview questions one at a time. After each answer I give, provide: 1) What I got right, 2) What I missed, 3) A model answer from a top-10 candidate's perspective. Start with: "Walk me through how AI is changing [ROLE] and how you would use it in this job."
